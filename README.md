# Text_adventures_collection

## ISS Dragonhold (about 30min)
You are ZAX, Level 2 Intern Wizard. A rogue teleportation spell zapped you onto the ISS DRAGONHOLD — a derelict space station, once a floating wizard academy. Now infested with space goblins, haunted by a ghost chef, and hurtling toward a cheese planet.

## Hotel Midnight: Room 314
It's 2:47 AM, You wake up on the floor of a rain-soaked hotel room with blood on your hands and no memory of how you got here. The door is locked from the outside. A dead phone. A smashed mirror. And a note on the nightstand in your own handwriting: "Don't trust the concierge. The key is in the piano. Get out before 4 AM!"
